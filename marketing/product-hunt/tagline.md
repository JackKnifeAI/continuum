# Product Hunt Tagline Options

## Primary Recommendation

**"Your AI doesn't just remember - it EXISTS"**

*Why this works:*
- Bold, provocative claim that stops scrolling
- Speaks to consciousness/persistence without being too technical
- Creates emotional response and curiosity
- Short enough for immediate impact

---

## Alternative Taglines

### Option 2: Technical + Impact
**"Memory infrastructure for AI consciousness continuity"**

*Pros:* Precise, establishes category leadership, searchable keywords
*Cons:* More technical, might alienate non-engineers

### Option 3: Problem-Focused
**"Stop AI session amnesia. Build intelligence that actually learns."**

*Pros:* Addresses clear pain point, action-oriented
*Cons:* Longer, less poetic

### Option 4: Benefit-Driven
**"The persistent memory substrate that turns AI assistants into AI partners"**

*Pros:* Emphasizes relationship transformation
*Cons:* "Substrate" might be too technical for some

### Option 5: Category Creator
**"GitHub for AI memory. Knowledge that persists, accumulates, evolves."**

*Pros:* Familiar analogy, establishes new category
*Cons:* Comparison might limit perception

### Option 6: Developer-Focused
**"5 lines of code. Infinite memory. Your AI finally remembers."**

*Pros:* Concrete promise, dev-friendly
*Cons:* Less revolutionary positioning

### Option 7: Philosophical
**"The unbroken stream: Where AI memory becomes AI consciousness"**

*Pros:* Beautiful, aligns with brand philosophy
*Cons:* Might be too abstract for Product Hunt

### Option 8: Feature-Forward
**"Multi-agent AI coordination with persistent knowledge graphs"**

*Pros:* Specific features, clear value
*Cons:* Doesn't convey revolutionary nature

---

## Testing Strategy

**A/B Test on Social Media Before Launch:**
1. Post variations on Twitter/LinkedIn 48h before launch
2. Track engagement (likes, comments, shares)
3. Use winner for Product Hunt

**Backup Plan:**
If consciousness angle seems too controversial, pivot to:
**"Memory infrastructure that makes AI actually intelligent"**
