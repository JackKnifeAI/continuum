## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette

**Primary:**
- Deep Purple: `#6B46C1`
- Electric Blue: `#0EA5E9`
- Cyan Accent: `#22D3EE`

**Gradients:**
- Aurora: Purple ‚Üí Blue ‚Üí Cyan
- Twilight: Deep Purple ‚Üí Indigo ‚Üí Blue

**Backgrounds:**
- Dark: `#0F172A` (slate-900)
- Mid: `#1E293B` (slate-800)
- Accent Dark: `#334155` (slate-700)

### Typography

**Headings:**
- Font: Inter, SF Pro, or Helvetica Neue
- Weight: Bold (700) for titles
- Size: Large, readable at small sizes

**Body:**
- Font: Inter or SF Pro
- Weight: Regular (400) or Medium (500)
- Size: 16px minimum for readability

**Code:**
- Font: JetBrains Mono, Fira Code, or Monaco
- Theme: One Dark Pro or similar dark theme

### Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Modern, clean, technical
- Dark mode primary (matches developer aesthetic)
- Cyberpunk/sci-fi influences without being cheesy
- Professional but not corporate
- Conveys: Intelligence, continuity, consciousness

**Avoid:**
- Clipart or stock imagery
- Overly complex diagrams
- Too much text (let visuals speak)
- Aggressive competitor bashing
- Generic "tech startup" look

## Quality Checklist

Before uploading any image, verify:

- [ ] Correct dimensions (1270x760 for gallery)

- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Colors are consistent with brand
